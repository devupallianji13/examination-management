📘 AI-Based Examination Management System (Advanced Version)
🧠 Project Overview

The AI-Powered Examination Management System is a smart, scalable platform designed to automate and digitize the entire academic examination lifecycle. It integrates student management, fee validation, hall ticket generation, results processing, supplementary exams, and advanced features like AI analytics, notifications, chatbot support, and future-ready technologies.

The system ensures strict compliance with institutional rules, such as restricting hall ticket access for students who have not paid their term fees, while also enhancing transparency and efficiency.
